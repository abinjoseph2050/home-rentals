Welcome to Cabins in the Bayou

Our story begins with the founders, Darrell and Barbara Edgley, who hand built the cabins that you could be staying in! The original idea for cabins on the bayou was a place for plant workers to stay while away from home for company trips. Currently we still rent to many plant workers, as well as people looking for a home away from home, or even long term residents; we thrive to give every resident a comfortable and reliable stay with us.

Things you may like to know before staying with us

  • We have an open laundromat on the property

  • We provide internet

  • We have community grills and fire pits for you to use at your leisure

  • No pets allowed (esa and service anaimals are accommodated )

  • All units come fully stocked with dining utensils, pots and pans, as well as a set of towles and toiletries for the bathroom

  • We offer full sized cabins as well as smaller units in fourplex

  • We offer week to week payments for rent or month to month

  • All units 2 beds 1 bathroom second beds are located in a loft

  • All units come with one parking spot more can be requested at no charge

  • Our fourplex units are 285 sq ft, and our cabins are 390 sq ft
